About Us

Tamarjy

Tmarjy is a medical services provider that offers a quick and efficient solution to identify medical conditions based on symptoms provided by users. The idea for Tmarjy was born out of my personal experience of suffering from pneumonitis (lung inflammation) when I moved to a new region. The lack of easily accessible medical services, complicated by the confusing medical specialties, posed a significant challenge. As someone who knows the value of timely medical attention, I realized the need for a software solution that could cut through the clutter and deliver reliable guidance. And that's exactly what Tmarjy does.
